## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### üéØ **Positive Behavior**

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our
community include:

- **Being respectful** of differing viewpoints and experiences
- **Gracefully accepting** constructive criticism
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community
- **Showing empathy** towards other community members
- **Helping others** learn and grow
- **Being patient** with newcomers
- **Giving credit** where it's due
- **Celebrating successes** of others
- **Sharing knowledge** freely
- **Being collaborative** and inclusive

### üö´ **Unacceptable Behavior**

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment** of any kind
- **Trolling** or inflammatory comments
- **Personal attacks** or insults
- **Spam** or unsolicited advertising
- **Off-topic** discussions that disrupt the community
- **Discriminatory** language or behavior
- **Doxxing** or sharing private information
- **Threats** of violence or harm
- **Sexually explicit** or inappropriate content
- **Intentional disruption** of community discussions
- **Abuse of power** or position

## üèóÔ∏è **Enforcement Guidelines**

### **Reporting Issues**

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

- **GitHub Issues**: Tag maintainers in a private issue
- **Discord**: Message any moderator or admin privately
- **Email**: conduct@saasbuilder.com
- **Anonymous**: Use our anonymous reporting form

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

### **Consequences**

Unacceptable behavior will result in:

1. **Warning** - Private warning with explanation
2. **Temporary Suspension** - Temporary removal from community
3. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from all community spaces

### **Appeal Process**

If you believe a consequence is unfair:

1. **Submit appeal** to conduct@saasbuilder.com
2. **Review by different maintainers** - Fresh perspective
3. **Decision within 7 days** - Prompt resolution
4. **Final decision** - Maintainers' decision is final

## üìö **Community Guidelines**

### **Communication**

- **Be clear and concise** in your messages
- **Use appropriate language** for all audiences
- **Stay on topic** in discussions
- **Listen more than you speak**
- **Ask questions before assuming**

### **Collaboration**

- **Give credit** to others' work
- **Seek consensus** before making major changes
- **Be open to feedback** and suggestions
- **Help newcomers** get started
- **Share your knowledge** generously

### **Technical Contributions**

- **Follow the style guidelines** for code
- **Write clear documentation** for your changes
- **Test your work** before submitting
- **Be responsive** to feedback on your PRs
- **Learn from mistakes** and improve

### **Community Spaces**

#### **GitHub**
- **Keep issues focused** on specific topics
- **Search before creating** new issues
- **Use appropriate labels** for categorization
- **Be constructive** in code reviews
- **Acknowledge and thank** contributors

#### **Discord**
- **Use appropriate channels** for different topics
- **Avoid @mentioning** everyone unless necessary
- **Be patient** waiting for responses
- **Help answer questions** if you know the answer
- **Keep conversations** respectful and on-topic

#### **Discussions & Forums**
- **Read before posting** to avoid duplicates
- **Provide context** for your questions
- **Follow up** on your own threads
- **Mark solutions** when problems are solved
- **Be encouraging** to others learning

## üåç **Inclusivity Commitment**

### **Language and Accessibility**

- **Use inclusive language** that welcomes everyone
- **Provide alt text** for images when possible
- **Consider time zones** when scheduling events
- **Provide transcripts** for audio/video content
- **Use clear formatting** for readability

### **Cultural Sensitivity**

- **Respect different cultures** and perspectives
- **Be aware of cultural holidays** and observances
- **Avoid culturally specific references** without context
- **Be mindful of language barriers**
- **Welcome diverse viewpoints** and experiences

### **Accessibility**

- **Ensure content is accessible** to people with disabilities
- **Use proper contrast** in visual materials
- **Provide captions** for video content
- **Consider screen reader** compatibility
- **Be flexible** with different needs

## üéØ **Leadership Responsibilities**

### **Maintainers**

Community leaders are responsible for:

- **Clarifying and enforcing** our standards
- **Leading by example** with positive behavior
- **Responding promptly** to reports
- **Being fair and impartial** in decisions
- **Educating the community** on expectations
- **Creating safe spaces** for participation

### **Moderation**

- **Remove inappropriate content** quickly
- **Warn users** about behavior issues
- **Escalate serious issues** to maintainers
- **Document all actions** taken
- **Communicate decisions** clearly
- **Learn from each situation**
