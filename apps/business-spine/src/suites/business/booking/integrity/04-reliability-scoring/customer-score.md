CUSTOMER RELIABILITY SCORE (INTERNAL)
=====================================

Signals:
- no_show_rate
- late_cancel_rate
- payment_failure_rate
- disputes_per_booking
- refund_rate

Uses:
- require prepayment/deposit
- enforce stricter cancellation window
- disable "instant book" for high-risk
- manual review flags

Output:
- score 0-100
- risk_band + reasons
