CONFLICT RESOLUTION FRAMEWORK
=============================

Goals:
- De-escalate
- Preserve trust on both sides
- Avoid chargebacks and public complaints

Resolution paths:
1. Clarification (misunderstanding)
2. Adjustment (partial refund/credit)
3. Reschedule
4. Platform goodwill credit
5. Formal dispute escalation

Tone rules:
- Neutral language
- No policy citations in first response
- Empathy before explanation
