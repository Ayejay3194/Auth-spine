## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us:
- Remember your preferences
- Improve your experience
- Analyze how you use our platform
- Keep your account secure

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)
These cookies are necessary for the platform to function:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Maintains your logged-in state | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `tenant_id` | Identifies your organization | Session |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie preferences | 1 year |

**You cannot opt out of essential cookies** as they are required for basic functionality.

---

### 2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies (Optional)
These cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform:

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Track page visits and user behavior | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics | Throttle request rate | 1 minute |
| `sentry_session` | Sentry | Error tracking and monitoring | Session |

**You can opt out** of these cookies through our cookie consent banner.

---

### 2.3 Functional Cookies (Optional)
These cookies enhance functionality and personalization:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|---------|----------|
| `theme_preference` | Remember dark/light mode | 1 year |
| `language` | Remember language preference | 1 year |
| `timezone` | Remember timezone setting | 1 year |
| `dashboard_layout` | Remember dashboard customization | 6 months |

**You can opt out** of these cookies, but some features may not work as expected.

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

## 4. How to Manage Cookies

### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner
- When you first visit, we display a cookie consent banner
- You can accept all, reject optional cookies, or customize preferences
- Your choice is saved for 1 year

### 4.2 Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser:

**Chrome:**
Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

**Firefox:**
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

**Safari:**
Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

**Edge:**
Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

### 4.3 In-App Settings
Go to: Account → Privacy → Cookie Preferences

## 5. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not respond to DNT signals at this time, but you can control cookies through our consent banner and browser settings.
